About MDS


At Maneuver Driving School, we’re committed to helping both new and experienced drivers develop the essential skills, confidence, and road awareness needed to drive safely and responsibly in all conditions.


Based in Vancouver, we are a fully licensed and ICBC-certified driving school (Driver Training School Licence #3280). With patient, professional instructors, well-structured lessons, and modern teaching techniques, we make learning to drive clear, simple, and stress-free.


Whether you’re a teenager preparing for your very first license, an adult looking to refresh your driving skills, or a newcomer to Canada adapting to local traffic laws, our training programs are tailored to match your learning style and pace.
